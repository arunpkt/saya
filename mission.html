Sri Sarvamangala Sayaputhiran Saneeswara Bhagavan Mission

Our Mission is to construct a temple dedicated to Saniswara Baghavan, it must be necessary for every devotee to give an iota of contribution for the construction of this temple.

The foundation stone for the temple was laid on 05.06.2015. The present Managing Trustee Shri K. Manikandan S/o Shri G.K. Murthy (The Author & Founder of Trust) and a large number of devotees participated inthe function. Shri ShaniBaghavan seated on Eagle as his vechicle and on the backside Shri Narayani has been carved as per agama sastras. The Temple is proposed to construct within a period of 5 years. A separate temple for Shri Shani Baghavan and following temples are proposed to construct in the Temple.

Then Artist Mr. G.K. Manikandan, has taken the responsibility of building the temple.

Chief Deities of Temple: Sri Saneeswara Bhagavan Important Shrines: Sri Sundara Ganapati, Sri Sarvamangala Adhipuri Easwari, Sri Sarvamangala Adhipuri Easwarer, Sri Valli Devanai Samadey Sri Muruga Peruman, Sri Lakshminarayanani Samadey and Sri Lakshmi Narayani, Sri Dhakshinamoorthy , Sri Kalabairavar, Sri Viswabrahma, Sri Gayathri Devi and Sri Mandhi

Important Shrines - Twelve Rasis: Sri Mesham, Sri Rishabham, Sri Mithunam, Sri Kadagam, Sri Simmam, Sri Kanni, Sri Viruchigam, Sri Dhanusu, Sri Magaram, Sri Kumbham and Sri Meenam.

Important Shrines - TwentySeven Nakshatras : Sri Aswini, Sri Bharani, Sri Karthigai, Sri Rohini, Sri Mrigasheersham, Sri Thiruvaathirai, Sri Punarpoosam, Sri Poosam, Sri Aayilyam, Sri Makkam, Sri Pooram, Sri Uthiram, Sri Hastham, Sri Chithirai, Sri Swaathi, Sri Visaakam, Sri Anusham, Sri Kettai, Sri Moolam, Sri Pooraadam Sri Uthiraadam, Sri Thiruvonam, Sri Avittam, Sri Chathayam, Sri Poorattathi, Sri Uthirattathi and Sri Revathi Other ongoing temple Project : Annadhana Koodam, Bhrindavana Gosala. Veda Pada Salai, Community Hall and Marriage Hall

The above temple will be unique in India, adhering to all religious directions Agama Sastras followed for constructing a temple. It will be built with donations from India and worldwide. It will be a place of worship without any reservation of caste, creed and religious. It will be a place of worship for one and all.

Participating in the noble work of constructing Arulmigu Shri Saniswara Baghavan Temple annihilates all sins, bestows immense punya and assures mukthi not only to the individual but also to ancestors. We welcome all devotees to take part in the construction work of the above mentioned Temple and Sannadhis.
