Unique feature of the Lord Shani Bhaghavan Temple

With the blessings of Kanchi Kamakodi Mahaperiyava and with public welfare in view the founder Thiru.G.K.Murthy intended to construct a special temple for the first time in the world for Lord Sri Saneeswara Bhagavan.

The unique feature of this temple is that of the statue of Sri Saneeswara Bhagavan which is installed here is designed in such a way that attracts everybody. Thiru.G.K.Murthy founder of the Sri Jayasayaa Charitable Trust and Sri Sarvamangala Sayaputhiran Sevaimaiyam Charitable Trust played a prime role for designing the statue which was purely imagined by him. Another notable feature of this statue is that of the sculpture of Narayani which his sculpted on the back of the statue. Narayani is the consort of Narayana who is the primordial energy of this world. The Sun is referred to Narayana and his consort is referred to Sayadevi as Shani Bhaghavan was born to them, he is called Sarvamangala Sayaputhiran Shani Bhagavan.

Narayani is consorted of Narayana. Narayani is the primordial energy of the world. Shani Bhaghavan is the personification of his energy. The Sun is Narayana. Once Narayani goes to see his father while going she leaves her shadow known as Sayadevi in her place. In course of time Sayadevi gives birth to two children one is Sayaputhrian by name and other is Yama.

Shani Bhaghavan is the most powerful god who punishes or rewards him/her according to his/her Karma. Generally people get afraid of Shani Bhagahvan because if we approach him, they think they might get trouble in various forms. People need not be afraid of Shani Bhaghavan. He is always compassionate and ready to help people relieve from sufferings. If they pray to him sincerely the sufferings will be mitigated.

The concept of designing the statue of Lord Sri Saneeswara Bhagavan (unique one) is to bring sufferers here to avoid miseries by eulogising him (God). He is not one who gets carried away by platitudes but by being humble one can achieve success. Here the God Sri Saneeswara Bhagavan is seated as the personification of success.

The Special feature of the temple is to indicate that 27 Stars, 12 Rasis and Navagrahs are unified in this temple. Sri Saravamangala Sayaputhiran Saneeswara Bhagavan is like a Karpagavirutcham who along with Naryanai bestows wealth and benediction on every devotee who comes and has darshan of Sayaputhiran and Naryanai seated in the temple. At the most 6000 devotees come and if each one donates Rs. 5000/- it will help the trust complete the construction of the temple successfully.

If a devotee involves himself/herself in the noble service of completing the construction of the temple for the Lord Sri Saneeswara Bhagavan, Bhagavan will take a seat in his/her a heart and bestow his blessing on him/her. Besides this he/she will live long life (120 years) with prosperity.
