History of Sri Sarvamangala Sayaputhiran Sri Saniswara Bhaghavan Temple

Throughout the Universe diverse creations in countless forms along with the oceans, Mountains, flora and fauna thrive b a Divine Order of Destiny. These mortals are happy or sorrowful as per their "KARMA" and as ordered by primordial force the "ADI SHAKTHI"

As per Cosmic order SHE apportions our life with shares of happiness and anguish. A study of ancient scriptures and writings of Astrology clearly reveal the cosmic plan that the Omnipotent "Devi" orders these dispensations not directly but only through the Navagrahas.

As such with the thought of the Great Guru in heart, as ordained by the karma of the founder trustee Shri G.K. Murthy, Journalist Artist began this world's wonderful exciting but exacting task of building this temple. Quarter of a century ago the vision of the Great Saniswara who appeared in his Mind's Eye Egged him on to ask the Sage of Kanchi, the Paramcharya of Kamakotipeetam whether he could launch his project appeared in his vision. Before he could proceed further he had the answer from the Mahaswami of kanchi. He smiled and told him "you are trying to achieve the impossible but you have the blessings of Sri Sarvesvara and you will achieve your goals. You will realise your dream in due course but wait a little and then you will get the help from Government and the public." The Swamiji blessed him and gave him Tulsi and Nagalinga flower with his sacred hands.

Out of the nine planets it is the Lord Saturn who is the Natural Giver of Gifts or otherwise and He is the only "Ishvara" among the nine. When the Lord Saniswara is on top, you go up in your ladder of fame, but when he climbs down naturally you are pulled down also. This is the order of the Global Divine Plan. The Jothisha Shastras give clear indications of the masterful Power of the Saturn.

When people are caught in his web of fate it will be in their interests to worship the all-powerful Lord Saturn in an Idol form. In the Entire celestial our country Bharat holds the key to spirituality. It has the prime of place in this Globe. Due to huge increase in population and other complication, our trials and tribulations have increased to an intolerable degree. The only way out of this is to worship the Navagrahas for succour.

Talking of the powerful Lord Saturn all the astrological indices point to the fact that He is as generous as He could be cruel. As an evidence of this fact our attention is invited to the sufferings of Emperor Nala and Pandava Brothers. When such great souls suffered under the spell of this Iswara what about us, mere mortals?

"Though we have myriad of prayers and hymns eulogising Lord Saniswara, there seems to be no separate Temple for this Lord or any correct description of Him. As such after going through various ancient texts the founder presumed a figure for Sri Sanishwara.

In today's Kali age, many people may be able to do Pooja, Japa, Homa with gifts, Parayana etc., but quite a few may not be able to afford this. Perhaps many may not be able to adopt correct Shastric procedures through a proper Guru.

Only with this public welfare in view, the founder carefully drew up the Sacred Portrait of the all-pervading Lord Saturn. In addition to this he also intended to construct a special temple for the first time in the World for Lord Saniswara by the munificence and sincere co-operation of the people like Messrs Naidu Hall. We have in this context various plans and he requested all large hearted friends to lend a helping hand to this very noble cause and get the Blessings of Lord Saniswara.

He therefore entreated everyone of this strife torn world to worship the sacred feet of Lord Saniswara and attain solutions to their problems and get the ultimate bliss.

It will be an unique temple in India, adhering to all religious directions Agama Sastras followed for constructing a temple it will be built with donations from India and abroad. It will be a place of worship without any reservations of caste, creed and religion. It will be a place of worship for one and all. It will be built in vast area. It has also been planned to help the poor and needy for their basic education, medical facilities, and industrial requirements. A 6 feet idol in black granite has been sculpted. The work will be completed in the next five years. The construction cost is estimated to RS. 15,00,000,000 ( Rupees Fifteen Cores). We request the religious minded general public, the elite and industrialists to kindly donate for this noble cause of constructing this unique temple.
