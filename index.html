Introductory Note

Among the navagrahas, Sri Saneeswara Bhagavan is the only God holding the Easwaran appellation. Sri Saneeswara Bhagavan is considered as the God who makes and breaks fortunes by his influence and position in the planetary system for which he is especially worshipped. There are myriad of prayers and hymns eulogizing Lord Sri Saneeswara Bhagavan, one among the nine planets. There is hardly any separate temple for this Lord, nor any correct description of him.

How we Launched the Holy Project

  • The mission was started as early as during the year 1993. Shri.G.K.Murthy is the Founder Trustee for the concept of constructing - Exclusive temple for Lord Shani Bhaghavan.

  • The mission has been functioning since 2015.

  • Sri Saniswara Bhavan statue work completed and is ready for worship at Padagacherry village near Alangudy Gurusthalam at Thiruvarur District at its own place.

  • Construction site of the temple has been recently provided with Electricity facility.

  • After the completion of innovative architectural plan for Lord Shani Bhaghavan temple. The construction work started.

  • Now the construction work is going in full swing.

  • Devotees are requested to activity participate, donate liberally for the on-going temple projects which will annihilates all sins, bestows immense punya and assures mukthi not only to the individual but also to ancestors.

Commitment to Fruitful Result

Offering help we could for a good cause is called Punniya (Meritorious) Karma. Temple Renovation, Reconstruction, Kumbabhishekam, etc. are all considered extremely noble deeds. We all should do whatever we can with respect to this and earn Punniya Karma. Whatever we offer, it may be a brick, but as long as that brick is there in that temple, we will spend that much time (thousands of years) in Kailsash or Vaikuntham. Kanchi Maha Periyava
